Welcome to Little Ducklings Childcare!

Hours of operation: Weekdays, 7:30AM ∼ 5:30PM

We are a fully licensed family child care located in a quiet area of Downtown Kamloops.

We offer:

  • Reasonable rates
  • Flex and part time schedules
  • Meals and snacks
  • Organic, whole grain, and unprocessed foods
  • Cloth diaper accommodation
  • Library visits
  • Nature walks
  • A program based on the LifeWays approach

What is the LifeWays approach?

LifeWays programs recognize that human relationships and connection to the living world of nature are the essential tools for all foundational skills for life.

  • Domestic arts: Children find joy in the practical and meaningful activities of daily life, laying a foundation for future academic skills, initiative and purpose.
  • Nurturing arts: Children are nourished with natural foods, gentle care, daily outdoor time, and rest.
  • Social arts: Mixed age groups foster a secure relationship with the primary caregiver over time and provide the opportunity to be both a younger and older “sibling.”
  • Creative arts: Imaginative play is enriched by story telling, artistic activities, crafts, music and singing.
